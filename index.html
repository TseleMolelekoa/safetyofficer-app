What to Report
  1. Explain what happened - Provide a clear description of the incident
  2. Indicate where it happened - Specify the exact location
  3. Indicate when it happened - Provide date and time of the incident
  4. Explain who is / was involved - Identify all persons involved
  5. Explain how it happened - Describe the sequence of events
